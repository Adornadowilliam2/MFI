MFI CREED

MFI Creed We believe in S.E.R.V.E. as the element that can bind MFI into a cohesive excellent organization.
We believe that the disadvantaged should be given preferential attention and concern.
We believe that in order to serve our beneficiaries and society best, we have to be an excellent organization.
We believe that an excellent organization depends on the people comprising it.
We believe in respecting the rights, enhancing the dignity, and nurturing the personal and professional development of each individual.
We believe in managing the resources of MFI prudently and judiciously so it can continue to serve more and better.
We know that MFI exists to serve the common good.
